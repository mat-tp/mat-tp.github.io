Project 4: Time Traveler's Guestbook

Embark on a journey through time with the Time Traveler's Guestbook! This unique web application allows you to leave your mark across the temporal plane, registering your presence in the timestream and viewing fellow time travelers who have passed through. Witness the current temporal coordinates on a mesmerizing clock and even earn temporal ranks as you make multiple journeys through time. Built with Python, Flask, and other web technologies, the guestbook offers a sleek, modern interface with responsive design, ensuring compatibility across various devices. Explore the past, present, and future – all in one place!

Language: Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS

Tools: Flask, Node.js
